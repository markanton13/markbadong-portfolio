## 8. About preview
Background narrative plus location, target roles, working style, and current direction.

Direct invitation for remote operations, virtual assistance, CRM, automation, quality, and systems opportunities.

## 10. Footer
Brand summary, GitHub, LinkedIn, email, and copyright.
